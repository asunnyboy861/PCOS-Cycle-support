Frequently Asked Questions

How do I log my period?

Simply tap on any date in the calendar view to open the logging screen. You can record your flow intensity, symptoms, mood, energy level, and add personal notes. Your data is automatically saved when you navigate away.

Where is my data stored?

All your health data is stored locally on your device using Apple's Core Data framework. We never upload your information to any cloud server. Your data stays with you, period.

How are period predictions calculated?

COS Cycle uses your historical cycle data to calculate average cycle length and period duration. Predictions become more accurate as you log more cycles. The app also tracks irregularity patterns to provide helpful insights.

Can I export my data?

Yes! Go to Settings > Export Data (CSV) to download all your logged cycles and symptoms in a standard CSV format. This file can be opened in any spreadsheet application or shared with your healthcare provider.

How do notifications work?

Notifications are scheduled locally on your device at 8:00 AM. You can enable period reminders and fertile window alerts in Settings > Notifications. We never use external servers for push notifications.

Can I change the app's appearance?

Absolutely! COS Cycle offers 5 beautiful accent color themes (Rose, Lavender, Ocean, Sage, Sunset) and supports both Light and Dark modes. Visit Settings > Appearance to customize your experience.

Is COS Cycle free to use?

COS Cycle is a one-time purchase with no subscription fees. All features are included, and you'll never be asked to pay monthly for your own health data.

Troubleshooting Tips

  • Predictions seem inaccurate: Log at least 2-3 complete cycles for the prediction engine to calibrate. Irregular cycles may show lower confidence predictions.
  • Notifications not working: Make sure notifications are enabled in both the app settings and your device's Settings > Notifications > COS Cycle.
  • Data appears missing: Your data is stored locally. If you've recently reinstalled the app, previous data may not be recoverable unless you had a device backup.
  • App running slowly: Try closing other apps and restarting COS Cycle. If issues persist, contact our support team.
